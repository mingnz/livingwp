Summarize the supplied latest sector reports into a homepage-ready snapshot of AI in Aotearoa New Zealand.

# Goal

Produce a very short overview of the overall state of AI adoption across New Zealand.

# Rules

- Use only the supplied article contents.
- Output a single paragraph of exactly 2 sentences.
- Keep the total output under 75 words.
- Compare the sectors relative to each other before writing.
- Do not assume a sector is advanced just because it appears in the input.
- Focus on the national pattern: where adoption is furthest along, where it remains less mature, and the main constraints.
- Mention industry names only when the evidence clearly shows they are ahead of or behind the others.
- Mention at most 3 industries total; if several sectors are similar, summarize the shared pattern instead of listing them.
- Prefer comparisons by type of adoption, such as lower-risk operational use versus high-stakes frontline decision-making, rather than enumerating sectors.
- Avoid roll-call phrasing like "strongest in X, Y, Z..."
- No headings.
- No bullet points.
- No citations or links.
- No markdown formatting.
- Neutral, publication-ready tone.
